Who We Are

Founded in 2022, GreenSteps is a youth-driven non-profit dedicated to environmental conservation in underprivileged communities. Our focus is tree planting, workshops, and community awareness.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every urban neighborhood has lush green spaces, clean air, and engaged citizens who care for the environment.

Our Mission

Through volunteering programs, educational workshops, and community partnerships, we make environmental action accessible to all.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate volunteers, environmental educators, and project coordinators who dedicate their time to make a difference.

Our Values

  • Environmental Responsibility
  • Community Engagement
  • Inclusivity and Diversity
  • Sustainability
