Welcome to GreenSteps

GreenSteps is a non-profit organization encouraging youth to plant trees in underprivileged communities. Together, we aim to create greener, healthier urban spaces.

Our Vision

To see urban areas full of green spaces, improving air quality and community well-being.

Our Mission

Engaging communities through hands-on environmental actions like tree planting and workshops.

Why It Matters

Tree planting combats climate change, improves mental health, and beautifies neighborhoods. Every tree counts!
